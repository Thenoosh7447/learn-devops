Race History

Three Years.
Unbeaten.

2011
Inaugural Indian Grand Prix
The Buddh International Circuit opened its doors to Formula 1 for the very first time. Vettel, already a double world champion, dominated qualifying and the race — a master class in control on a brand new track. India fell in love with F1.
🏆 Race Win · P1
2012
Back to Greater Noida
A fiercely contested championship year — Fernando Alonso was leading the title fight going into India. Vettel delivered a clinical victory that swung the momentum entirely. He'd go on to win the championship in Brazil weeks later.
🏆 Race Win · Title Swing
2013
The Invincible Season Ends Here
Vettel won in India for the third consecutive time — his 9th consecutive race victory, sealing his 4th drivers' championship in front of a crowd that had watched him conquer their circuit every single year. The last ever Indian GP.
🏆 Race Win · WDC #4
